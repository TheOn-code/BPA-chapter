What is BPA?

What is BPA?

 The BPA year kicks off with the Fall leadership conference (FLC) in November. Here you will network with other chapters and learn business related skills all while having a great time. After FLC you will decide what events you will work on. There are a huge variety of individual and team events ranging from Healthcare to business to programming. In February you go to the State Leadership Conference (SLC) to compete in your events. If you place high enough at state, you are given the opportunity to compete at nationals. This year's nationals is in Anaheim, California.
