Events

 Events are what you will spend the majority of your time on in BPA. On this page you can explore some of the event options and what people have done in the past for them. Click on any of the event boxes below to learn more!

Presentation Management

 Presentation management asks you to research a given topic then create a presentation to go along with it and present it to a judge.

Entrepreneurship

 In entreprenuership you get to design the business of your dreams. You will develop and write up a business plan and a presentation to go with it.

Website Design Team

 Website design team asks you to create a website on a given topic. You will then present your website along with an explanatory presentation to a judge.

Small Business Management Team

 In this event you are given a business scenario and tasked with coming up with solutions to whatever the problem is at hand.

UX Design

 Unfortunately this event no longer exists but you can you use their presentation for inspiration.

Broadcast News Production Team

 You will create a 5 minutes news broadcast on the topic that is given to you.

Presentation Management Team

 Create a presentation as a team on the assigned topic
