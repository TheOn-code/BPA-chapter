Leadership

 The West High BPA chapter is run by the executive board. On this page you can meet the exec board and get to know them better.

Karen

 Meet Karen! Karen is a senior and has been in BPA for four years and three years on the Executive Board. This is Karen’s second year serving as a state officer and is currently the State President of Iowa BPA. One thing that Karen loves about BPA is the lifelong friendships she has gained through this club and the connections she has built with her advisors. Outside of BPA you can find her working at Everbowl making delicious smoothie and acai bowls or at the pool training for the 2024 Olympics…just kidding. Karen is so grateful to have joined BPA her freshman year and developed an amazing support system.

Dillon

 Meet Dillon! Dillon is a senior and is going into his third year in BPA. This year will also be his second year serving on the Executive Board. In addition to returning on the Executive Board, Dillon is serving as the West High Chapter President. One thing Dillon especially loves about BPA is the connections you make with the other members in the chapter and across the nation. When he is not spending time in BPA, Dillon stays busy with competitive golf, swim team, drumline in the marching band, eating, and managing his many fantasy football teams.

Jack

 Greetings from Jack! Jack is a junior and will be starting his second year in BPA and first year on the Executive Board. Jack loves BPA as it gives you opportunities to meet and collaborate with like-minded individuals from your own school and all across the nation. Jack is also the manager of Pump it UP! West BPAs student run Cafe, he is hoping to open back up sometime this school year. Outside of BPA you will find Jack working at the Coralville Hy-Vee or around town with friends. Jack is super excited to start the new year in BPA and can’t wait to meet all of the members!

Praneel

 Meet Praneel! Praneel is a sophomore going into his second year of BPA, and it’s his first year on the Executive Board. One thing Praneel loves about BPA is the numerous opportunities it provides across the board. From tough competitions to fun meetups, he has enjoyed every step along the way. Outside of BPA he is a passionate runner who is known to run too many miles. He finds his stride by pushing his limits, embracing challenges, and helping and connecting with others through the sport that he loves. Praneel can’t wait for this upcoming BPA year and all the fun things planned.

Audrey

 Meet Audrey! Audrey is a sophomore starting her second year of BPA and first year on the Executive Board. One thing Audrey loves about BPA is the connections you make with others, competing in events, and all the soft skills you learn in between! You can often find her playing tennis or working out at the HTRC. She enjoys road biking, skiing Copper Mountain, or even just napping. Audrey is thankful for her past experiences in BPA and is thrilled for the upcoming year!

Myra

 Meet Myra! Myra is a sophomore kicking off her second year of BPA and her first year on the Executive Board. One thing Myra loves about BPA is all of the opportunities it gives you to get outside your comfort zone, experience new things, and grow as a person. In addition to BPA, Myra enjoys swimming, hiking with her family, beach vacations, and Costco muffins. Myra is so grateful for all of the opportunities BPA and her chapter advisors have given her, and is eager to get the 2023-2024 season underway!

Layan

 Layan is a sophomore and this will be her 2nd year in BPA. As a freshman, Layan competed at the National Leadership Conference in Dallas and placed second in the nation in her competitive event. As a first-year member of the Executive Board, Layan is excited to work on increasing productive awareness about BPA and recruiting new members to the club. Outside of BPA, Layan is involved in orchestra, volleyball, student government, and is a server at the Melrose Meadows Retirement Community. This year, Layan is looking forward to working on her competitive events and attending this year’s NLC in Anaheim, California.

Lucy

 Meet Lucy! Lucy is a junior going into her third year of BPA and her first on the Executive Board. Something Lucy loves about BPA are the lifelong relationships you make and the opportunities it gives you. As well as just BPA Lucy works at Maggie’s Farm Pizza, loves hanging out with her friends, and always has time for a coffee run! She is grateful for the lifelong memories she has made and the experiences she has gained through BPA, and she can’t wait for the upcoming BPA season!

Brooklynne

  Brooklynne is a junior, excited to start her third year in BPA and first year on exec board! Some of the things Brooklynne loves about BPA are the lasting relationships you build and the community that grows throughout the year. Outside of BPA, Brooklynne is on the Varsity Cheer team at West High, Co-Head of production for Theater West, and works at Maggie’s Farm Pizza. She loves to spend time with her loved ones and read in her free time. She is grateful for the opportunities BPA and her advisors have provided her so far and can’t wait to make many more memories!
