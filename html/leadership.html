Leadership

 The West High BPA chapter is run by the executive board. On this page you can meet the exec board and get to know them better.

John

 Greetings from John. John is a Senior and this is his fourth year in BPA and 3rd year on the Executive Board, serving on the meetings committee this year. John is the State President of Iowa BPA and is looking forward to working with his team this year to make Iowa BPA as fun and beneficial as possible for all members. Outside of BPA he likes to be very active through biking, running, swimming and lifting. He also enjoys being outdoors and computer programming. His favorite part about BPA is meeting new people from all over the country and is always there as a resource for anyone who needs help with BPA.

Karen

 Say hello to Karen! Karen is a junior this is her third year in BPA, second year on the executive board and first year as a BPA state officer. This year Karan is excited to help plan the United Fall Leadership Conference combining with FBLA and DECA and to compete at state and nationals in California. Apart from BPA, Karan has been swimming for 9 years and is a hostess at Tribute eatery & bar. Karen’s favorite parts about BPA are the friendships that she has gained and the lifelong relationships she has built with her advisors.

Mohana

 Salutations from Mohana! Mohana is a senior and this is her third year doing BPA and in fact her 2nd year on the Executive Board. She is helping lead the membership committee and is looking forward to meeting new people from all over the country, building more connections, and competing on the state and national stage. While Mohana isn’t working for BPA, you can often find her playing tennis, working out, volunteering at the hospital, or working in the lab. She has an unlimited amount of curiosity so she is eager to learn and succeed in her events in BPA.

Himani

 Meet Himani! Himani is a senior and has participated in BPA for 2 years. This is her first year on the executive board. Within the executive board, she is leading the marketing committee and is looking forward to Nationals in Anaheim, California during the spring. In addition to BPA, Himani is involved in speech and debate, student government, and is the 2022-2023 MERGE Youth Entrepreneurship Ambassador. Himani’s favorite part about BPA is the authentic community and perfect balance between fun and competition.

Biswash

 BPA Biz in the house!! Biswash(Biz) has been in BPA for 2 years and is currently a senior. With his first year on the executive board he plans to grow BPA’s presence through his focus on membership and community. Making it to the Texas national stage, he is excited to continue the streak this year with a national title in Anaheim, California. Outside of BPA, Biz is passionate about finance, real estate and Investing. Biz’s favorite aspect of BPA is the environment of knowledge. From the competitions to the networking events he loves that BPA teaches you real world skills to help you grow your professional career.

Dillon

 This will be Dillon’s 2nd year in BPA and first on Executie and is currently a Junior. Dillon is excited to be back competing for West BPA. In Dillon’s first year as a sophomore, Dillon and his team placed 10th at the national competition in Dallas. Hoping to build on last years success, Dillon is ready for the competition season to start. Outside of BPA, Dillon has been a golfer his entire life and competes in tournaments around the midwest. Dillon also swims for West High during the winter when it's too cold to golf. Dillon’s favorite part of BPA is the connections you can make and the experiences you can have at competitions.

Layan

 Layan is a sophomore and this will be her 2nd year in BPA. As a freshman, Layan competed at the National Leadership Conference in Dallas and placed second in the nation in her competitive event. As a first-year member of the Executive Board, Layan is excited to work on increasing productive awareness about BPA and recruiting new members to the club. Outside of BPA, Layan is involved in orchestra, volleyball, student government, and is a server at the Melrose Meadows Retirement Community. This year, Layan is looking forward to working on her competitive events and attending this year’s NLC in Anaheim, California.

Mitch

 Meet Mitch! Mitch is a senior going into his second year of BPA and is a brand new member on the exec board. Mitch is a part of the membership committee and hopes to see plenty of new faces at our meetings this year. Besides BPA, Mitch is involved in football, baseball, and hopes to kick off his bowling career this winter. Mitch’s favorite part of BPA is the connections and friendships you form with new people. He will always be around the school and be happy to answer any questions about BPA that you might have!

Eva

 Meet Eva! Eva is a senior and this is her third year in BPA and first year on the Executive Board. This year, Eva is looking to build new connections and continue her journey all the way to Anaheim this Spring. Outside of BPA, Eva works for the West Side Story as a designer and wishes to continue to pursue design in the future. Along with designing, Eva is an avid reader and enjoys hypothetical discussions a little too much. You’ll also catch her on the football field during halftime playing with the Trojan drum line on Friday nights. Eva’s favorite part about BPA is the people you meet and the opportunities for self-growth through competition and leadership.

Zach

 Greetings from Zach! Zach is a senior and beginning his 3rd year in BPA and he is looking forward to a great year coming off of a phenomenal nationals trip, creating the chapter website and going to Anaheim in April. Outside of BPA, Zach has been playing piano for 10 years and enjoys playing ragtime and listening to all types of old music. He also loves to run with his friends as well as water-ski and wakeboard. Zach loves to learn and especially enjoys math and computer science. His favorite part of BPA is the conferences and working on events with his team.
